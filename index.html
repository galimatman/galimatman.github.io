About Me

My name is Dawn, and I have been a primary school teacher for over 20 years. I adore children and love to promote and assist their educational development. I offer private tuition in children’s homes, supporting their school experience.

My Services

Private In-Home Tutoring

I provide one-on-one, in-home tutoring sessions tailored to your child’s needs. I can come to any areas in Essex and Hertfordshire.

Progress Reporting

You can keep track of your child's progress through regular discussions with me and from my monthly reports. I advise the specific areas on which we have worked, the progress your child is making and the next steps. I link all comments back to the National Curriculum so that you can be sure that we maintain our progress and align with work your child is undertaking at school.
